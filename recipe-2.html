Deviled Eggs

Total prep time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • ½ tsp garlic salt
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 slices bacon
  • Olives (halved)
  • Small jalapeño slices

Instructions

  1. Boil eggs with salt. Cool under cold water and peel.
  2. Slice eggs lengthwise and remove yolks.
  3. Mix yolks with ingredients.
  4. Place jalapeño in egg white, add filling.
  5. Top with olive, bacon, and paprika.
