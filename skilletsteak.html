Skillet Steak

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Top Sirloin
  • 2 tsp grilling seasoning
  • 3 cups mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups frozen peas
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 6 oz broccolini
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp avocado oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Season steak and garlic.
  2. Heat oil in cast-iron skillet. Add steak and broccolini.
  3. Cook 4 minutes. Add peas. Transfer skillet to oven.
  4. Bake to 145°F (about 8 minutes). Remove steak.
  5. Whisk cornstarch and broth. Add mushrooms.
  6. Simmer and pour sauce over steak. Serve.
